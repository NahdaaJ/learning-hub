# Layered Architecture
Most projects are organised into the following layers:
- Presentation, 
- Application, 
- Domain,
- Infrastructure.

A restaurant analogy can be used to explain these layers.

## Presentation Layer
The presentation layer is the layer that the user sees and interacts with.
In C#, this can be seen as:
- a WPF project, 
- an Angular frontend

The presentation layer contains:
- buttons,
- forms, 
- view models, 
- controllers, 
- UI validation

>In the restaurant analogy, the presentation layer is the **waiter**.
> You tell the waiter what you want, they go tell the kitchen, and then they bring the food once its done.
> The waiter doesn't cook the food.
>**A presentation layer takes your input, calls the right service, then provides you with the result.**
